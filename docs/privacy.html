Privacy policy

Last updated: May 24, 2026

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Chill Flight ("Application") for mobile devices, which was created by Cowneck. The Application is a relaxing flight simulator game designed to provide a peaceful flying experience.

Information collection and use

1. Personal information

The Application does not collect or request any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, physical address, or phone number) during use. You can play the game completely anonymously.

2. Device sensor data

To control the airplane, the Application uses your device's built-in sensors (specifically the gyroscope and accelerometer) for motion controls.

  • Usage: This data is processed in real time, locally on your device, solely to translate physical device tilt into flight maneuvers.
  • Storage: No sensor or motion data is ever logged, stored, or transmitted off your device.

3. Automatically collected information

The Application may collect certain non-personal information automatically to ensure stability, track performance, and improve the user experience. This includes:

  • The type of mobile device you use.
  • Your mobile device's unique device ID.
  • The IP address of your mobile device.
  • Your mobile operating system.
  • Diagnostic information, such as crash logs and application performance statistics.

Third party services

The Application utilizes third-party services that may collect information used to identify you or analyze app stability. Below are the links to the privacy policies of the third-party service providers used by the Application:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase Crashlytics & Realtime Database

Data retention policy

We do not retain personal data because we do not collect it. For any automatically collected diagnostic data or multiplayer connectivity data (if applicable), it is managed by our third-party services (Google Play Services and Firebase) in accordance with their respective retention policies.

Children's privacy

This Application does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13 years of age. In the case we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we will be able to do the necessary actions.

Changes to this privacy policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.
